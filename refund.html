Refund Policy

This page explains the intended refund approach for Manga Translator Studio. Final refund terms should match your payment provider and applicable consumer law.

Important: this is a practical draft for the website. It is not legal advice. Review it with a qualified professional before accepting payments.

Free trial first

The free trial exists so users can test the full workflow with their own manga before purchasing a paid license.

Refund window

A reasonable launch policy is a limited refund window, for example 14 days, if the software cannot run on the customer’s system and support cannot resolve the issue.

Non-refundable cases

  • Repeated purchases after successful use.
  • Requests caused by unsupported third-party AI models.
  • Refund requests after extensive use or abuse.
  • Problems caused by copyrighted material, scan quality or unsupported workflows.

Subscriptions

Monthly, 6-month and yearly access should remain active until the end of the paid term unless your checkout provider states otherwise.

How to request a refund

Use the contact page and include your purchase email, order ID, operating system and a short description of the problem.
