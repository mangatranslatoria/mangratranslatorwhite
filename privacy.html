Privacy Policy

Manga Translator Studio is designed around local processing. Manga files and translations remain on your computer unless you choose to share them yourself.

Important: this is a practical draft for the website. It is not legal advice. Review it with a qualified professional before accepting payments.

Local processing

The application is built to run OCR, AI translation and rendering locally on your PC through local tools such as LM Studio and compatible AI models.

  • Your manga images are not uploaded by the application to our servers.
  • Translation prompts are sent to the local AI endpoint you configure.
  • If you use a third-party model, service or cloud endpoint, that provider may process data under its own terms.

Data we may collect

When payments, licensing or support are enabled, we may process basic account and transaction information such as email address, license status, purchase plan, payment provider references and support messages.

Payments

Payments should be handled by an external provider such as Stripe, Paddle, Lemon Squeezy or PayPal. Card numbers are processed by the payment provider, not by Manga Translator Studio directly.

License activation

License checks may send a license key, plan type, activation status, app version and limited device information needed to prevent abuse and manage activations.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, use the contact page. Replace this section with your final business name, address and email before launch.
